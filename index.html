Project




As a part of the Community Service Project, A Community Socio-economic survey was conducted by me and my team in order to identify the rate of increase in the occurence of the different cyber crimes and frauds around us.
This survey resulted in the identification of different cyber frauds and the rate of its impact on the oersonal information.During this survey, we understood the different types of cyber crimes and frauds that occur in the everyday lives of people.
Due to this survey, the people were able to understand the different types of crimes and the way to deal with them. We provided the various measures and precautions in which the people can solve these issues.
Based on the issue, we designed a web portal that provides information about the different cyber frauds and crimes and the ways in which these crimes or frauds shall be avoided and prevented.
All the data collected during the survey was used in the designing of this portal. This portal would help people to identify the different cyber crimes and frauds that take place around us.
The core technologies used to design this web portal are HTML and CSS. HTML provided the basic structure of the web page whereas CSS provided the design of the web page.
